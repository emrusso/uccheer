About

The cheer team at the University of Chicago attends home football games during the fall, and home men's and women's basketball games in the winter. We bring spirit to Maroon athletic events by cheering, stunting, and dancing! All skill levels are welcome on the team.

Follow us on social media by liking our Facebook page, or by following our Instagram!

Join

The UChicago cheer team is a co-ed sports club that cheers, stunts, and dances at home football and basketball games. We welcome all skill levels on our squad.

We are always looking for new talent to join our team! If you wish to join UC Cheer, please see our Instagram for information regarding open practices and tryouts or contact our captains at cheerleading@uchicago.edu for more information.

Donate

The UChicago cheerleading team receives some funds from the university's sport club allocations as well as from our own fundraising efforts on campus. However, these amounts do not fully cover the cost of our gear (uniforms, poms, megaphones, etc.) or the cost of attending competitions. Any help you'd be willing to provide would be deeply appreciated. Please leave us your name and address when you donate; we'd love to send you a cheerful thank you note!

We happily accept checks made out to "The University of Chicago" with UC Cheer written in the memo line, which can be mailed to the treasurer for depositing. For more information, contact our captains at cheerleading@uchicago.edu. The University can send a letter of acknowledgement to donors, thanking you for your contribution and making it possible to claim your charitable contribution on your taxes.
